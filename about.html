What is Catdopolis?

Catdopolis is a fictional cat adoption site. This site is designed to portray the fictional presence of Catdopolis, a cat adoption facility in the fictional town of Milson, Georgia. Catdopolis provides an array of beautiful, spunky, and loveable cats along with the portrayal of their potential adoption as a means of bringing attention to the wonderful act of adopting a cat. Adopting a cat can help not only the cat to have a successful life, but to also add to the love and quality of your own. The following information will be worded as if it was present on an actual site. Once more, I would like to remind you of this site's presence as a fictional entity.

Why Adopt?

Here at Catdopolis we encourage people to adopt because it can not only change the life of the cat for the better, it may also change the life of the adopter. Though cats may have a reputation with some as being moody, they are loveable and have a lot of love to give. Many cats do not find a home sometimes and are unfortunately left alone. Here at Catdopolis we have policies in place to ensure their longevity, however, we seek diligently to find them a home, otherwise they are transferred to one of our sister facilities or a foster home for extended care. Your fur baby may be here right now, just waiting on you.

The Process

1. Check for our available adoptees using our Adopt Now page, found here. You can also reach out to our team and we can offer suggestions based on what you are looking for.

2. Contact us and we will begin your application process. As well, we will determine a time that is best for you to meet with potential candidates.

3. Our team will then do an interview with you and a virtual or in home visit will be requested to ensure your home is ready for your kitty. It is important to us that you and our cats have a successful new life.

4. Once approved, there is an adoption fee of $160 per cat that is required. Vaccines are provided prior to adoption.

We appreciate your patience in this process. The success of your new family member is important to us. Our team will work dilligently to unite you with your fur baby. Some cats can be quite popular, so we do encourage promptness when you know your interests. We are confident this is the beginning of something great for you and your new fur baby.
